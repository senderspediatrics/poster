    When to Call Us

    • A rectal temperature of 100.4°F (38°C) or higher.
    • Significant trouble feeding or staying latched.
    • Persistent vomiting or trouble breathing.
    Call us right away if your baby:

    Will not wake to eat, seems truly lethargic, or has crying that cannot be consoled.
